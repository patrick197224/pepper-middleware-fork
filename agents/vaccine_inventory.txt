VACCINATION ADMINISTRATION

All vaccinations at GreenOak Family Practice are administered by Sarah Thompson, RN, under the supervision of the general practitioners. Vaccination appointments are typically scheduled as short nurse-only visits (15 minutes).

Before vaccination, patients should inform the clinic of:
- Any allergies, especially to vaccine components
- Current medications and medical conditions
- Previous adverse reactions to vaccines
- Pregnancy status (if applicable)

After vaccination, patients are asked to remain in the clinic for 15 minutes for observation in case of immediate adverse reactions.

VACCINE SCHEDULING

Vaccination appointments can be scheduled through:
- Telephone booking with the front desk
- Online booking system
- Same-day appointments may be available for routine vaccinations

For vaccines that are currently out of stock, the clinic staff can:
- Provide information about expected restock dates
- Assist with locating alternative vaccination locations
- Add patients to a notification list for when vaccines become available
- Coordinate with other healthcare facilities if urgent vaccination is needed
